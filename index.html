Our Story

01 — The Beginning

Where it started.

Gamaluxe started with one product and one problem: outfits that wouldn't stay put. We set out to make a body adhesive kit gentle enough for everyday skin, but strong enough to last through a full day — or a full night out.

What began as a small batch made in Lagos quickly became something women trusted for every occasion, from weddings to nights out, because it simply worked.

02 — The Expansion

Where it's going.

As the brand grew, so did the vision. Gamaluxe expanded into custom handmade bags — hand-crocheted, hand-beaded, and made to order for women who wanted something no one else would be carrying.

Different material, same philosophy: confidence and craftsmanship, made by hand, made to be noticed.
